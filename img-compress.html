Frequently Asked Questions

Will compressing my image reduce its quality?

Our tool uses smart algorithms to minimize quality loss while maximizing file size reduction. You can control the balance between quality and compression with the quality slider. For most web uses, even high compression levels produce excellent results.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG (best for photographs), PNG (best for images with transparency), and WebP (best compression). You can keep the original format or convert to another format during compression.

Is my image data secure?

Absolutely! All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.
